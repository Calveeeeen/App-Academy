Roles

Controllers

Controllers are delegated the role of providing cover and/or denying vision for the players in the game. They all have utility to deny vision by placing smokes on the map. While they all have a "smoking" ability, the way each character implements this ability is unique. As we go over each agent, we'll discuss each agents abilities, and how their smoking ability is implemented.

Astra

Ghanaian Agent Astra harnesses the energies of the cosmos to reshape battlefields to her whim. With full command of her astral form and a talent for deep strategic foresight, she's always eons ahead of her enemy's next move. Astra has four abilities: GRAVITY WELL, NOVA PULSE, NEBULA and DISSIPATE, and her Unique ability/Ultimate ability ASTRAL FORM/COSMIC DIVIDE. Even when comparing all the controllers with each other, Astra is uniquely different from the rest. With her unique ability Astral Form, Astra enters an Astral Form and is able to place "stars" on the map and with these "stars" Astra is able to activate any one of her abilities on these "stars". Gravity Well pulls players within the radius of the ability towards the center before it explodes. Any players trapped inside will become fragile and will take more damage for a short amount of time. Nova Pulse charges for a brief amount of time and will concuss all players within the radius of the ability. Concussed players will have vision blur, reduced movement speed, and slower fire-rate. Nebula is Astra's signature ability and her smoking ability. There will be a smoke placed where the "star" was placed and it will deny vision for a brief amount of time. Dissipate allows Astra players to retrieve their "star" to be placed somewhere else after a cooldown duration. When players retrieve their "stars" it'll leave a smoke that will last an even shorter duration than the Nebula ability. The inside of Nebula is hollow , which means that if 2 players were to walk into Nebula they will be able to see each other in the smoke. Astra's Ultimate Ability is called Cosmic Divide. You'll need to charge this ability up by killing enemies, planting the bomb or dying. Each of previous actions will provide the ability with 1 charge. Cosmic Divide creates an infinite wall that blocks bullets from going through and heavily dampens audio from the other side of the wall. However abilities are still free to travel through the wall.

Brimstone

Joining from the USA, Brimstone's orbital arsenal ensures his squad always has the advantage. His ability to deliver utility precisely and from a distance makes him an unmatched boots-on-the-ground commander. Brimstone has four abilities: Stim Beacon, Incendiary, Sky Smoke, and his ultimate ability Orbital Strike. Brimstone is the mosst straight forward of all the controllers, his kit is simple and as straightforward as it can get to use. Stim Beacon instantly tosses down a stim beacon. Upon landing, it creates a field that grants players a combat stim and a speed boost. Incendiary fires a grenade that detonates as it comes to a rest on the floor, creating a lingering fire zone that damages players within the zone. Some players create lineups with this ability so that they can fire it from a safe distance away from a bomb plant to safely win rounds for the team, others will find line ups to prevent the enemy team from planting the bomb. Brimstone's signature ability Sky smokes fires to set locations where Brimstone's smoke clouds will land, alternate fire to confirm, launching long-lasting smoke clouds that block vision in selected areas. The inside of sky smoke contains debris, making it hard for players inside to smoke to see each other until they're in close proximities. Brimstone's Ultimate ability is called Orbital Strike,like Asta's ability this ability will need to be charged via kills, deaths, and/or bomb plants/defuses. Orbital Strike launches a lingering orbital strike laser at a targeted location, dealing high damage-over-time to players caught within the area. This ability can be used as a safety net to prevent enemies from planting/defusing the bomb from a safe distance away or it may be used to prevent enemies from taking a certain chokepoint.

Harbor

Hailing from India's coast, Harbor storms the field wielding ancient technology with dominion over water. He unleashes frothing rapids and crushing waves to shield his allies and to pummel those that oppose him. Harbor's four abilities are: CASCADE, COVE, his signature ability HIGH TIDE, and his ultimate ability RECKONING. Harbor is the newest addition to the controllers, While his kit mainly focuses on denying vision, it can also be used to create opportunities for your team. Cascade equips a wave of water, when fired it sends a wave of water rolling forward and through walls. Reactivate the ability to stop the wall of water at it's current position, when players walk through or hit by the wave they are slowed for a short period of time. Cove fires a sphere of shielding water, fire to throw the orb normallying and alternate-fire to throw it underhand. Upon impact the sphere a destructible water shield that will block a certain amount of bullets. This ability can be used to smoke off chokepoints or can be used to protect an ally planter/defuser. High tide equips a wall another wall of water, fire to send the water forward along the ground. Hold the fire button to guide the water in the direction of your crosshair, passing through the world, spawning a wall along the water's path. Alternate-fire while bending to stop the water early, players that walk through or hit by the wall is slowed for a short duration. Like every other agent, Habor's ultimate needs to be charged via kills, deaths and/or bomb plants/defuses. Harbor's Ultimate Ability is called Reckoning, reckoning equips the full power of your artifact, fire to summon a geyser pool on the ground. Enemy players in the area are targeted by successive geyser strikes. Player caught within a strike are concussed, players that are concussed will have screen distortion (player view may be blurred and/or shaky), hearing impairment (temporary deafness), and movement impairment (players are slowed or partially restricted).

Omen

A phantom of a memory, Omen hunts in the shadows. He renders enemies blind, teleports across the field, then lets paranoia take hold as his foe scrambles to learn where he might strike next. Omen's four abilities are: SHROUDED STEP, PARANOIA, his signature ability DARK COVER, and his ultimate ability FROM THE SHADOWS. Omen's kit is the most versatile among the controllers. His abilities can be used more aggressively and/or passively depending on the player. Shrouded step allows Omen to teleports to a marked location after a brief channel. This ability can be used to get away from a dangerous situation, and/or used to aggressively take space to obtain a more favorable/unexpected angle to suprise enemies. Paranoia allows Omen to equips a blinding orb that travels through obstacles, fire to throw it forward in a straight line, briefly nearsighting and deafening all players it touches. Omen's signature ability Dark Cover, equips a shadow orb, entering a phased world to place and target the orbs. Press the ability key to fire the shadow orb to the marked location, creating a long-lasting shadow sphere (smoke) that blocks vision. The inside of Dark Cover is hollow, so players hiding inside the smoke can see anyone entering the smoke before they can see the player hiding in the smoke. Omen's Dark Cover ability is considered a projectile, which means that it can break some obstacles in the game. Omen players can use this neat trick on Ascent to fake position and catch enemies offguard on Ascent. Dark Cover is also the easiest smoking ability to create one ways, meaning that you can place smokes to your advantage where you can see enemy's lower portion of their body while from their perspective they can't see anything. Omen's ultimate ability like the others need to be charged via kills, death, and/or plant/defuses of the bomb. Omen's ultimate ability From the Shadows allows the player to teleport to a targeted location anywhere on the map. While teleporting Omen will appear as a Shade that can be destroyed by an enemy to cancel his teleport, or if omen reactivates his ability to cancel the teleport.

Viper

The American chemist, Viper deploys an array of poisonous chemical devices to control the battlefield and cripple the enemy's vision. If the toxins don't kill her prey, her mind games surely will. Viper's four abilities are: SNAKE BITE, POISON CLOUD, her signature ability is TOXIC SCREEN, and her ultimate ability is VIPER'S PIT. Viper's the only controller as of right now that has passive abilities. Her passive abilities are Fuel and Toxin. Viper's passive Fuel and Toxin work as a single passive. While fuel gradually generates and replenishes her toxins (passive ability) that fuel her abilities, toxin inflict enemies that come into contact with poison cloud, toxic screen, and vipers pit with decay. Players that are decayed lose 30 health instantly and then 10 health per second for every addition second they're in contact with the abilities mentioned above. Snake bite allows viper to launch a chemical canister that shatters upon hitting the floor, creating a lingering chemincal zone that damages and applies Vulnerability. Players that are Vulnerable take more damage from bullets and other abilities. Poison Cloud throws a gas emitter that perpetually remains throughout the round. Alternate-fire to lob the emitter. Re-use the ability to create a toxic gas cloud at the cost of Fuel. Poison Cloud can be use more than once and can be picked up to be redeployed. Viper players will generally place their Poison Cloud on top of the bomb so that it can be used in conjunction with her Snake Bites. This is because her snake bite ability by itself is not strong enough to kill an enemy that has full health, while using it in conjunction with the Poison Cloud, the decay helps amplify Snake Bite's damage. Vipers signature ability Toxic Screen equips a gas emitter launcher that penetrates terrain, it deploys a long line of gas emitters. Re-activate ability to create a tall wall of toxic gas at the cost of Fuel. This ability can also be used more than once. Viper's ultimate ability Viper's Pit needs to be charged like every other ultimate ability via kills, deaths, and/or bomb plants/defuses. Viper's Pit allows viper to equip a chemical sprayer. Fire the ability to spray a chemical cloud in all directions around the targeted area, creating a large cloud that nearsights players and decays the health of enemies inside it. Hold the ability key to disperse the chemical cloud early.

Duelists

Duelists are agents that are designated to take aggressive fights and put pressure on the enemy team. Their primary role is to enter sites and engage in combat, providing their team with early map control and fragging potential. They are typically equipped with abilities that allow them to take fights on their own terms and to push past enemy defenses. This makes them effective at creating openings and disrupting the enemy's setup. Duelists are often relied upon to secure early picks and to trade kills effectively. They require strong mechanical skills, game sense, and map knowledge to excel in their role. Each duelist has a unique set of abilities that complement their playstyle, and we'll explore these abilities in more detail as we review each agent.

Jett

Representing her home country of South Korea, Jett's agile and evasive fighting style lets her take risks no one else can. She runs circles around every skirmish, cutting enemies before they even know what hit them. Jett's Kit is the most straight forward of the duelists but also takes some time to get used to. Jett has five abilities: CLOUD BURST, UPDRAFT, her signature ability TAILWIND, her ultimate ability BLADE STORM and her passive ability DRIFT. Drift allows jett to glide through the air while falling by holding space button. This can help Jett players get on to elevated surfaces without the use of her updraft ability. Cloud burst allows Jett to instantly throw a projectile that expands into a brief vision-blocking cloud on impact with a surface. Hold the ability key to curve the smoke in the direction of your crosshair. Updraft propels jett upwards high into the air. Her signature ability tailwind prepares a gust of wind for a limited time. Re-use the ability to propel Jett in the direction that she's moving. If Jett is standing still she will be propelled forward. This ability is generally used as a repositioning tool when taking gun fights. It allows Jett to take an aggressive fight and get out if the fight is unfavorable for her. Jett's Ultimate ability needs to be charges via kills, deaths, and/or bomb plants/defuses before it can be used. Her Ultimate Ability Blade Storm equips a set of highly accurate throwing knives, fire to throw a single knife and recharge the set on kill. Alternate fire to fire all the knives but does not recharge on kill. Jett players can take favorable fights with Blade Storm by using her ultimate in conjunction with updraft, drift, and tailwind because of the high accuracy of Blade Storm.

Neon

Filipino Agent, Neon, surges forward at shocking speeds, discharging bursts of bioelectric radiance as fast as her body generates it. She races ahead to catch enemies off guard, then strikes them down quicker than lightning. While Neon's kit is also straight forward it's very difficult to use her abilities in conjunction. Neon's five abilities are: FAST LANE, RELAY BOLT, her signature ability HIGH GEAR, her ultimate ability OVERDRIVE, and her passive ability ENERGY. Energy is the fuel used to power Neon's abilities High Gear and Overdrive. Using those abilities gradually consume Energy and getting a kill instantly recharges Energy. Fast Lane allows Neon to fire two energy lines forward on the ground that extends a short distance or until they hit a surface. The lines rise into walls that block vision. Relay allows Neon to instantly fire an energy bolt that bounces off any surface once. Upon hitting each surface, the bolt shocks the area below and above with a concussive blast. Players that are concussed have blurred vision and shaky screen, slowed movement speed, and a slower fire rate. Neon's signature ability High Gear instantly channels Neon's powers for increased speed. When charged alternate-fire to trigger an electric slide. The slide recharges every 2 kills. If this ability is used with a expert proficiency in movement manuevers it makes Neon a very hard target to hit. Neon's ultimate ability needs to be charged via kills, deaths, and/or bomb plants/defuses. Neon's ultimate ability Overdrive unleashes Neon's full power and speed for a short duration. Fire to channel the power into a deadly lightning beam with high movement accuracy.

Phoenix

Hailing from the U.K., Phoenix's star power shines through in his fighting style, igniting the battlefield with flash and flare. Whether he's got backup or not, he'll rush into a fight on his own terms. Although Phoenix's curving flashing ability may take some time to master, once you become proficient in using it, he can be a highly valuable addition to any team composition. Phoenix's five abilities are: BLAZE, CURVE BALL, his signature ability HOT HANDS, his ultimate ability RUN IT BACK, and his passive ability HEATING UP. Phoenix's passive ability, Heating Up allows Phoenix to passively heal himself instead of taking damage when standing in his own fire. Blaze equips a flame wall, fire to create a line of flames that move forward, creating a wall of fire that blocks vision and damages players passing through it. Hold fire to bend the wall in the direction of your crosshair. Curveball equips a flare ball that takes a curving path and detonates shortly after throwing. Fire to curve the flare orb to the left, detonating and Blinding any players who see the orb. Players that are blind can't see anything on the screen for a short amount of time. Alternate-fire to curve the flare orb to the right. Phoenix's signature ability Hot Hands, equips a fireball that explodes after a set amount of time or upon hitting the ground, creating a lingering fire zone that damages enemies. Alternate fire to lob the fire ball instead of throwing it. Phoenix's ultimate ability Run It Back needs to be charges just like all the other ultimate abilities via kills, deaths, and/or bomb plants/defuses. Run It Back instantly places a marker at his current location, while this ability is active, dying or allowing the timer to expire will end this ability and bring Phoenix back to the location with full health and the amount of armor he had when the ability was cast.

Raze

Raze explodes out of Brazil with her big personality and big guns. With her blunt-force-trauma playstyle, she excels at flushing entrenched enemies and clearing tight spaces with a generous dose of “boom". Raze possesses the most explosive kit out of all the duelists, as her abilities revolve around explosives. She can utilize these explosives to propel herself forward or deal massive damage to enemies. Unlike the previously dueslists raze on have four abilities: BOOMBOT, BLAST PACKS, her signature ability PAINT SHELLS, and her ultimate ability SHOWSTOPPER. Raze's first ability equips a Boom Bot, when the fire button is clicked it will deploy a bot, causing it to travel in a straight line on the ground, bouncing off walls. The Boom Bot will lock on to any enemies in its frontal cone and chase them, exploding for heavy damage if it reaches them. Blast pack allows Raze to instantly deploy a Blast Pack that will stick to surfaces, reusing the ability after deployment will detonate, damaging and moving anything it hit. Raze players often use this ability to quickly traverse the battlefield and gain a tactical advantage by propelling themselves forward into a site or directly at the enemy. Raze's signature ability, Paint Shells, equips her with a cluster grenade that explodes on impact, releasing smaller grenades that also detonate after a brief delay, dealing damage to enemies caught in their blast radius. To use the ability, press the fire button to throw it directly, or press the alternate-fire button to lob it. The ability will recharge after obtaining two kills. Raze's ultimate ability like the other agents needs to be charged via kills, deaths, and/or bomb plants/defuses. Raze's ultimate ability, Showstopper, equips her with a powerful rocket launcher that deals massive area damage upon impact with any surface. Skilled Raze players often use this ability in conjunction with Blast Packs to gain a strategic advantage by achieving a better angle for maximum splash damage.

Reyna

Forged in the heart of Mexico, Reyna dominates single combat, popping off with each kill she scores. Her capability is only limited by her raw skill, making her highly dependent on performance. Reyna's play style is focused on aggressive pushing and individual fragging, allowing her to move quickly and eliminate enemies with ease. She is best suited for players who are confident in their abilities to secure kills and want to play a high-risk, high-reward style. Reyna's five abilities are: LEER, her signature abilities are DEVOUR and DISMISS, her ultimate ability is called EMPRESS, and her passive ability is called SOUL HARVEST. Soul Harvest provides Reyna with Soul Orbs if they die to her, or die within 3 seconds of taking damage from Reyna. These Soul Orbs are then used for her signature abilities. Leer equips Reyna with an etheral, destructible eye. Activate the cast the eye a short distance forward. The eye will nearsight all enemies that are have the eye in their field of view. Reyna signature abilities are Devour and Dismiss. Devour instantly consumes a nearby soul orb, rapidly healing Reyna for a short duration and can be disrupted by taking damage. Health gained through this skill exceeding 100 will decay over time. If her ultimate is active, this skill will automatically cast and will not consume the Soul Orb. Dismiss instantly consumes a nearby Soul Orb, becoming intangible for a short duration, while intangible Reyna cannot use and abilities/weapons and is immune to damage. If her ultimate is active, she will also become invisible when the ability is activated. Reyna's ultimate ability Empress needs to be charges via kills, deaths, and/or bomb plants/defuses. While Empress is active, Reyna instantly enters a frenzy, gaining a Combat Stim (faster fire rate and reload speed). She also gains infinite charges on her signature abilities. When an enemy dies to Reyna, or dies within 3 seconds of taking damage from Reyna, the duration of Empress is renewed.

Japanese native Yoru rips holes straight through reality to infiltrate enemy lines unseen. Using deception and aggression in equal measure, he gets the drop on each target before they know where to look. Yoru is the most disruptive and sneaky of the duelists. His abilities revolve around him distracting his opponents. Yoru like Raze doesn't have a passive. His four abilities are: FAKEOUT, BLINDSIDE, his signature ability GATECRASH, and his ultimate ability DIMENSIONAL DRIFT. Fakeout allows Yoru to equip an echo that transform into a mirror image of Yoru when activated. Fire to instantly activate the mirror image and send it forward. Alternate-fire to place an inactive echo. Use your interact key to transform an inactive echo into a mirror image and send it forward. Mirror images explode in a Blinding Flash when destroed by enemies. Blindside equips an unstable dimensional fragment from reality. The fire button throws the fragment, activating a flash that winds up once it collides with a hard surface. His signature ability Gatecrash equips a rift tether, fire to send the tether forward, alternate-fire to place a stationary tether activate the ability to teleport to the tether's location, use the interact key to trigger a fake teleport. Yoru's ultimate like the others needs to be charged via kills, death, and/or bomb plants/defuses. Yoru's ultimate ability Dimensional Drift equips a mask that can see between dimensions. The fire key allows Yoru to drift into his dimension, unable to be affected or seen by enemies from the outside. In Dimension Drift, Yoru players can use Gatecrash to teleport to a different site and retrieve the bomb if it was dropped in a hard-to-reach location surrounded by enemies. This allows for a quick and unexpected plant while also preventing the risk of trading lives to retrieve the bomb.

Initiators

initiators are agents whose primary role is to start or initiate a fight with the enemy team. They use their abilities to gather information on enemy positions, create space for their team to move in, and make it difficult for the enemy team to hold a position. Although all initiators have the same role, their abilites vary so that their playstyles are very different from each other. We'll discuss the abilities in more detail as we review each agents.

Breach

Breach, the bionic Swede, fires powerful, targeted kinetic blasts to aggressively clear a path through enemy ground. The damage and disruption he inflicts ensures no fight is ever fair. Breach's playstyle revolves around creating opportunities for his team to take control of a site or gain an advantage in a firefight. His abilities that allow him to disrupt enemy positions and open up new angles of attack. Breach's four abilities are: AFTERSHOCK, FLASHPOINT, his signature ability FAULT LINES, and his ultimate ability ROLLING THUNDER. Aftershock equips a fusion charge into breach's arm and fires a slow-acting burst into any surface. The burst does heavy damage to anyone caught in its area. Flashpoint equips a blinding charge. Fire the charge to set a fast-acting burst through any surface. The charge detonates to blind all players looking at it. His signature ability Fault Lines equips a seismic blast, hold fire to increase the distance, release to set off the quake, concussing all players in its zone and in a line up to the zone. Breach's ultimate ability like the other ultimate abilities needs to be charges via kills, deaths, and/or bomb plants/defuses. Breach's ultimate ability Rolling Thunder equips a seismic charge. Fire button sends a cascading quake through all terrain in a large cone. The qukae concusses and knocks up anyone caught inside the zone. Players that are concussed have their vision blurred, movement speed slowed, and fire-rate slowed for a brief duration.

Fade

Turkish bounty hunter Fade unleashes the power of raw nightmare to seize enemy secrets. Attuned with terror itself, she hunts down targets and reveals their deepest fears - before crushing them in the dark. Fade's playstyle specializes in pinpointing enemy locations as well as flushing them out. Her utlities revolve around marking a target and causing distress by deafening any players hit with her utility. Fade's five abilities are: PROWLER, SEIZE, her signature ability HAUNT, her ultimate ability NIGHTFALL, and her passive ability Terror Trail. Her passive ability applies a terror trail that marks enemies, causing their position and movement to be tracked by dark mist. Prowler can be used to pursue trails. Fade's first ability prowler sends a "prowley" forward, hold the fire button to steer the prowler in the direction of your crosshair. The prowler will chase down the first enemy or terror trail that it sees, and nearsight the enemy on impact. Her ability seize throws a knot of raw fear. The knot drops down after a short period of time, however you can drop the seize early by reactivating the ability. The knot ruptures on impact, holding nearby enemies in place. Enemies that are held in place are Deafened and Decayed (loses a set amount of health that is later regenerated if no damage is taken while decayed). Her signature ability Haunt, throws a haunting watcher that drops down after a set amount of time, you can drop the haunt earlier by reactivating the ability. The haunting watcher lashes out on impact and reveals enemies in its line of sight and creating terror trails to each enemy revealed. Enemies can destroy the watcher. Fade's ultimate ability like the others needs to be charged via kills, deaths, and/or bomb plants/defuses. Nightfall equips the power of nightmare itself, and unleashes a wave of unstoppable nightmare energy. Enemies caught in the wave are marked by terror trails, deafened, and decayed.

His signature ability sends Dizzy soaring forward through the air. Dizzy charges then unleashes plasma blasts at enemies in line of sight. Enemies that are hit with her plasma are blinded. Enemies that are blinded by Dizzy will have goo-like substance on their screen for a brief period. Gekko's ultimate ability ultimate ability like the others needs to be charged via kills, deaths, and/or bomb plants/defuses. Thrash links Gekko to Thrash's mind and steer her through enemy territory. Activate to lunge forward and explode, detaining any players in a small radius. Gekko's passive allows Wingman, Dizzy, and Thrash to revert into a dormant globule after they are used. Gekko then has a brief period where he can interact with the globules to reclaim them and gain another charge of the ability after a short duration. Thrash can only be reclaimed once.

Sova

Born from the eternal winter of Russia's tundra, Sova tracks, finds, and eliminates enemies with ruthless efficiency and precision. His custom bow and incredible scouting abilities ensure that even if you run, you cannot hide. Sova's playstyle revolves around gathering information and providing support to his team. His abilities are focused on reconnaissance and disruption, allowing him to locate enemies and weaken their positions. Sova's four abilities are: OWL DRONE, SHOCK BOLT, his signature ability RECON BOLT, and his ultimate ability HUNTER'S FURY. Sova's Owl Drone ability deploys and takes control the movement of a drone to scout areas for enemies and can even fire a dart that will reveal enemies for a brief period of time if hit. Shock Bolt fires an arrow that upon contact with any surface will detonate an explosive shock that will damage enemies that are caught within it's range. You can add up to two bounces on this ability and holding the fire button will charge up the range of the projectile. Sova's signature ability Recon Bolt shoots a destructible arrow from his bow that can attach to any surface. Upon landing, it will reveal enemies caught in the line of sight of the arrow. Hold down the fire button to draw the bow further back and increase the power of the shot. Releasing the button fires the arrow. Additionally, you can add two bounces to this ability, similar to the Shock Bolt with the alternate-fire button. Sova's ultimate ability like the others needs to be charged via kills, deaths, and/or bomb plants/defuses before it can be used. Hunter's Fury equips Sova with a bow that can fire three long-range, wall-piercing energy blasts in a straight line in front of Sova. Hunter's Fury will damage and reveal all enemies that are caught in the line of fire for a short period of time. This ability can be fire three times total, and each energy blast deals massive damage to the enemies hit.

Sentinels

A Sentinel is a defensive-oriented agent whose primary role is to protect the team's designated sites or areas. They do this by using their abilities to slow down or deny enemy movements, gather information, and provide cover for their teammates. Sentinels typically play a supportive role, staying back and holding down key positions on the map with traps, barriers, and alarms. They often act as the anchor of the team, providing the backbone of the defensive strategy and preventing the enemy from gaining too much ground.

Chamber

Well dressed and well armed, French weapons designer Chamber expels aggressors with deadly precision. He leverages his custom arsenal to hold the line and pick off enemies from afar, with a contingency built for every plan. Chambers abilities as a sentinel differ from the rest. His kit allows him to play as a more aggressive type of sentinel. His four abilities are: TRADEMARK, HEAD HUNTER, his signature ability RENDEZVOUS, and his ultimate ability TOUR DE FORCE. Chamber is classified as a sentinel due to his ability Trademark. Trademark places a trap that scans for enemies on the floor. When a visible enemy comes in range, the trap counts down and destabilizes the terrain around them, creating a lingering field that slows players caught inside of it, while also notifying your team that someone has set your trap off. Trademark can be picked up to be redeployed during the round. Headhunter equips Chamber with a heavy pistol that can be scoped in with alternate-fire. His signature ability Rendezvous places down a teleporting anchor on the ground. While Chamber is on the ground and within the range of Rendezvous, reactivating the ability will quickly teleport Chamber to the anchors position. Rendezvous can be picked up to be redployed during the same round. Chamber players will generally take advantage of this ability by taking an aggressive peak, getting a kill, and then teleporting out. Chamber's ultimate ability like the rest of the agents needs to be charged via kill, deaths, and/or bomb plants/defuses before he can use it. Tour de Force equips Chamber with a custom sniper that will kill enemies with any direct hit to the upper body and up. killing enemies with Tour de Force will create a lingering field that slows players caught in the field.

Cypher

The Moroccan information broker, Cypher is a one-man surveillance network who keeps tabs on the enemy’s every move. No secret is safe. No maneuver goes unseen. Cypher is always watching. Cypher's playstyle revolves around setting up traps and gathering intel on enemy movements. His abilities allow him to create a web of traps and cameras across the map, giving him and his team a significant advantage in terms of information. Cypher's four abilities are: TRAPWIRE, CYBER CAGE, his signature ability SPYCAM, and his ultimate ability NEURAL THEFT. Trapwire places a destructible covert tripwire at the targeted location, creating a line that spans between two surfaces (there's a limited range). Enemy players who cross the tripwire will be tethered (unable to move to a certain extent), revealed (highlighted through smokes, walls, and other obstacles), and concussed after a short brief period of time if they don't destroy the device in time. This ability can be picked up to be redeployed in the same round. Cyber Cage instantly tosses a device on the floor in front of him. This can be activated to create a zone that blocks off vision and plays an audio cue when enemies pass through it. The inside of this cage is hollow, therefore players inside the smoke can see each other. His signature ability Spy Cam places a covert camera at a targeted location, Cypher is limited to how far away he can place the cam from his current location. Re-use the ability to take control of the camera's view. While in control of the cam, fire to shoot a marking dart, this dart will reveal the location of any enemy hit by the dart until the dart is taken off. His ultimate ability like the others need to be charged via kills, deaths, and/or bomb plants/defuses before it can be used. Neural Theft can only be used on a targeted enemy corpse to download information on their team's position. After a brief delay, the location of all living enemy players will be revealed. The location will be revealed again after another short delay.

Killjoy

The genius of Germany, Killjoy secures the battlefield with ease using her arsenal of inventions. If the damage from her gear doesn't stop her enemies, her robots debuff will help make short work of them. Killjoy's playstyle is focused on setting up traps and holding positions. Her abilities allow her to secure areas and deny enemy entry. Killjoy's abilities are: NANOSWARM, ALARMBOT, her signature ability TURRET, her ultimate ability LOCKDOWN. Her first ability equips Killjoy with nanoswarm grenades that can be thrown, alternate-fire to lob them. Upon landing the nanoswarms go covert. Reactivate the ability to deploy a swarm of nanobots that deal damage to players that are caught inside the area of effect. Alarmbot places a covert robot that hunts down enemies that enter its range. After reaching the target it will detonate and apply vulnerability to enemies in the area. This robot can be picked up for redploy in the same round. Her signature ability turret places down a turret that will fire at enemies in a 180 degree cone

Sage

The bastion of China, Sage creates safety for herself and her team wherever she goes. Able to revive fallen friends and stave off aggressive pushes, she provides a calm center to a hellish fight. Sage is a supportive agents that can be tricky to play against. She excels at healing and crowd control. Her playstyle revolves around providing healing and protection to her team while slowing down and hindering the enemy team's movements. Her four abilities are: BARRIER ORB, SLOW ORB, her signature ability HEALING ORB, and her ultimate RESURRECTION. Barrier orb places a wall that fortifies after a few seconds. Alternate-fire to rotate the orientation of the wall.
